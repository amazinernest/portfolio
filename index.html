About me

Story Time

My Tech Journey began in 2012 when I convinced my parents to buy me a laptop solely for gaming purposes.However, as I grew older, my curiosity led me to explore the laptop’s potential beyond gaming. It was like discovering magic when I started using Adobe Photoshop to create simple graphics, inspired by a friend’s basic photo manipulation techniques. In 2018, amidst the widespread buzz about learning to code, I decided to take the plunge. Armed with a few downloaded YouTube videos, I embarked on a journey to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, gradually building my skills and creating basic web pages. Then, in early 2020, during the height of the pandemic, I stumbled upon WordPress and its remarkable efficiency in swiftly constructing websites. Given my prior understanding of HTML and CSS, I quickly grasped the intricacies of WordPress, allowing me to create websites more efficiently than ever before. In the late 2020s, I discovered Figma when a client requested a website prototype. Since then, I have fallen in love with Figma and am continuously amazed by its capabilities, which greatly enhance my website designs. As a freelancer, I constantly seek opportunities to expand my skill set. I find inspiration and valuable insights by exploring content on platforms such as YouTube, Twitter, Medium, and search engines. I am passionate about my journey in the tech industry, and I look forward to utilizing my diverse skill set to deliver exceptional results in every project I undertake.
